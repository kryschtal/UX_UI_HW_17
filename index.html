ABOUT

Hello I am Edward (Eddie) Kryschtal.
I am a freelance UX/UI designer.

With a background of Art and Psychology and work experiences in a variety of fields. I specialize in assessing gaps in a design or a platform while finding solutions to make an experience memorable and smooth. I enjoy designing independently and in a team setting.

I take inspiration from nature and different cultures. I particularly enjoy working with nonprofits and organizations with the goal of improving communities and having a positive global impact. Within those environments I hope to provide them with tools and ideas that they had not considered.

SKILLS

User Research


I have a knack for user research, it is easy to connect with people in interviews and make them feel at ease to allow them to share their thoughts and pain points.

User Flow


It is easy to find pain points when assessing a site or a platform. I find it important to fully understand not only what the designers want the users to accomplish but how the users tend to find their way to it.

Visual Design


I find it important to have my own voice with design but it also necessary to be able to help match up to others vision of a project to provide the correct feelings and connections.
